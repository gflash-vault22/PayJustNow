Digital Stokvel
Save together · Earn together
A stokvel is a trusted group savings tradition. We've made yours digital — your group saves monthly, the pool earns ~8.6% per year in Smart Saver, and payouts happen automatically. No cash, no spreadsheets, no trust issues.
Choose your type
🔄
Rotational (Merry-go-round)
One member gets the full pot each month
Everyone contributes the same amount each month. The full pool rotates to one member — their "turn" comes around once per cycle. Great for a big lump sum when you need it.
Pool earns 8.6% p.a. 2–20 members From R200/month
📈
Savings / Investment
Everyone saves together, shares interest at year-end
The whole group saves into one shared pool all year. At year-end everyone gets their money back plus their share of the interest. Great for disciplined saving with a reward at the end.
Pool earns 8.6% p.a. 2–20 members From R200/month

How your money earns
Contributions go into Smart Saver

Every rand contributed is invested via Vault22 Smart Saver — earning ~8.6% p.a. instead of sitting idle.

Interest shared fairly

Each member's share of interest matches their contributions. The more you put in, the more you earn.

Payouts within 24 hours

When it's your turn (Rotational) or year-end (Savings), funds settle back to your PJN Wallet within 24 hours.
